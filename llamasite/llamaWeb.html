The Hope for Humanity:

Why do you say that llama's are the hope for humanity? Are they really that amazing?

"Llamas are a symbol of peace. Just their very presence can grace a war-torn country into one of unity and harmony. With just a radiant grin, a llama can warm the cold, bitter hearts of enemies and allow compassion to develop. This is because a llama's majestic grin is irresistible and contagious. This grin, although seemingly simple, is a sign of happiness. When we're happy, we (as humans) are more likely to do good, and in the end, we are able to step closer to an ideal world with no violence."

Okay, but llamas can't solve the problem of scarce supplies for a growing population.

"Wrong. Llamas can actually emit light when they are perched upon a cliff. Similarly to how a llama's grin has the ability to transform the human condition, a llama can generate light when standing proud. What can you do with this light? Convert it into energy! You could grow plants during night time! Not only that, but you could absorb that light into solar panels! What's the science behind this? You see, sometimes you just have to wait because some answers are just waiting to be discovered by wonderful scientists around the world."

Wow! I'm totally convinced that llamas are the hope for humanity. Tell me more!

"Awesome! It's people like you who help make the world a better place. You're capable of believing, and when you're capable of believing, you have the power to change.
